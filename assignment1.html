The Origins of Donald Duck

The origins of Donald Duck's name may have been inspired by Australian cricket legend Donald Bradman. In 1932 Bradman and the Australian team were touring North America and he made the news after being dissmissed for a duck against New York West Indians. Walt Disney was in the process of creating a friend for Mickey Mouse when he possibly ready about Bradman's dismissal in the papers and decided to name the new character"Donald Duck".
